About Us

We are an indigineous and Lagos based fashion house with over 10years experience in the fashion industry.
Here at House of Olamide, we take time to make sure that you feel happy with your appearance and we deliver
to you the best tailor-made clothes just for you. We 'cut n sew' for a perfect fit. Our designs are detail-oriented
and are in tune with the latest trends in Fashion & style.

We source for fabrics from a variety of local and international manufacturers.
We are more than just a fashion house but we also run a series of charitable fashion shows
where we showcase our latest designs and collections.
