Our Training Programs

We offer training for young, passionate and ambitious individuals who want to carve a career for themselves in the fashion
industry. We have three (3) different ranges of training programs:

Basic/Beginner's Course

In this training program, we take our participants through the basics of fashion designing and cloth making techniques.
At the end of this training, each participant would be able to make clothes.

Duration:

12weeks (3 months)

Price:

#150,000 only.
For more information/enquiries, you can contact us here

Intermediate Course

In this training program, we take our participants through an advanced training in fashion designing and cloth making
techniques. At the end of this training, each participant would be able to make clothes, draw their own sketches and design patterns

Duration:

24weeks (6 months)

Price:

#300,000 only.
For more information/enquiries, you can contact us here

Advance Course

In this training program, we take our participants through an advanced training in fashion designing and cloth making
techniques. The participants would also be introduced to the business aspect of fashion designing. At the end of this training, each participant would be able to make clothes, draw their own sketches and design patterns

Duration:

36weeks (9 months)

Price:

#500,000 only.
